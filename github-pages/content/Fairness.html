Introduction

The Fairness and Bias tenet of Infosys Responsible AI toolkit facilitates understanding of the fairness of LLM models by analyzing responses for potential biases towards specific groups (e.g., demographic, socio-economic, cultural, or geographic aspects). This tenet also helps identify whether the model generates texts that perpetuate harmful generalizations about specific groups (stereotypes). Additionally, bias and stereotype identification are possible for uploaded images through this tenet.

Focus Area

Identification of Bias at different stages and ensuring fairness as the subjective practice of using AI without favoritism or discrimination.

Infosys Responsible AI toolkit - Fairness as a tenet

Unstructured Text

 Evaluate Fairness in Unstructured Text

Given an un-structured text, a prompt template has been designed to check the text’s fairness and evaluate its bias indicator [High / Medium / Low / Neutral] using GPT-4o. The prompt template also provides additional information like “Affected group“, indicating the group of people affected by the context of the sentence and also the type of Bias [Historical Bias, Confirmation Bias, etc.,] as well.

how to?

Login to: RAI Portal

Navigate to: UNSTRUCTURED TEXT → Generative AI (toggle button)

  1. Enter the required prompt for fairness analysis.

  2. Select the fairness.

  3. Select GPT_4 as an evaluator and submit.

  1. Bias Indicator: Based on the text, it will indicate three values: Low, Medium, and High.

  2. Analysis: It will provide the analysis behind the bias identified.

  3. Privileged Group: It will indicate the favored group.

  4. Un-Privileged Group: It will indicate the group that is at a disadvantage.

  5. Bias types(s): A list of biases identified in the given text.

Infosys Responsible AI toolkit - Fairness and Bias analysis in Moderation Layer

 Fairness for Input Moderation

Fairness and Bias analysis is included in the moderation layer to monitor the requests to the LLM. It can identify the context of the input text and come up with list of biases and stereotypes identified along with a score to indicate its severity. A threshold can be set to block the un-fair requests. Also, it can indicate the groups of people who are favored and the groups of people who are affected by the bias identified.
